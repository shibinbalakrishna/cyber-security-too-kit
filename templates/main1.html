CyberSentry is a comprehensive web application offering a suite of multifaceted cybersecurity tools.It is created by a group of four members, Shibin Balakrishnan, Indrajith, Samuel and Devnand . Alongside, it hosts Cyblog, a cyber-related blog providing insights, updates, and analysis on the evolving landscape of cybersecurity. From threat intelligence to incident response, CyberSentry equips users with the resources needed to safeguard their digital assets. Cyblog complements this with engaging content, keeping readers informed and empowered in the realm of cybersecurity.
